Our commitment to authenticity

Authenticity is at the core of every collectible we offer. At Sportxst, all items are obtained through exclusive, private signing sessions with our trusted partners. We do not source memorabilia from public events, third parties, or unverified channels.

Each item includes:

  • Tamper-proof certificate of authenticity
  • Unique identification number for verification

  • Access to online verification records

Every collectible is fully traceable and verified, giving you complete confidence in your purchase.
