Shipping & Delivery

At Sportxst, we understand that when you order authentic, signed basketball memorabilia, secure and dependable delivery matters just as much as authenticity. That’s why we partner with trusted carriers and use protective packaging to ensure your collectible arrives safely and in perfect condition.

1

Secure & Protective Packaging

All signed basketball items are carefully prepared for shipping:

  • Professionally framed pieces secured (where applicable)
  • Acid-free materials used for protection
  • Reinforced packaging to prevent damage in transit

Every collectible is handled with the attention it deserves—from our team to your door.

2

Global Delivery Coverage

We ship to collectors worldwide, delivering authentic basketball memorabilia across domestic and international locations. Shipping options and costs are clearly shown at checkout for full transparency.

3

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation. Once shipped, you’ll receive tracking details to follow your order every step of the way.

4

Tracked & Insured Shipping

All deliveries include tracking and insurance for added peace of mind. Delivery times may vary by location, but most domestic orders arrive within a few business days after dispatch.

5

International Orders

For international shipments, customs duties or taxes may apply depending on your country. These charges are the responsibility of the buyer, and delivery times may vary due to customs processing.

6

Trusted Delivery Partners

We work with reliable shipping partners to ensure your collectible arrives safely and on time. If you have any questions about shipping or delivery, our support team is always available to assist.
