Privacy Policy

At Sportxst, protecting your personal information is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or make a purchase.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and site usage (via cookies)

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Process and deliver your orders

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze how visitors interact with our site. These help enable core features and improve performance.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Sharing Your Information

We only share your data when necessary with trusted service providers, such as:

All third parties are required to handle your information securely and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. While no system is completely secure, we take reasonable steps to safeguard your data.

Data Retention

Your personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Policy Updates
